True Advisory Discussions

True Advisory Discussions are a great way to stay connected with your classmates, tutors and TAs outside of the classroom. You can search for classes you are currently enrolled in and join conversations with your classmates. You can also ask tutors or TAs questions, stay up to date on class deadlines, and have private discussions for group projects. You will never have to worry about missing an assignment deadline or waiting a week to have your question answered again!

Your One-Stop Shop for Tutoring, Advising, and Course Discussions

Welcome to True Advisory

True Advisory is a one-stop shop for tutoring and course discussion. Add your courses to your course list, and you can see the course discussion and available resources for that course. Users can submit updates to the page if the information is outdated, especially so students can stay up-to-date with what they need to succeed. There are a few problems that True Advisory seeks to solve. Currently important resources are scattered around the main UMD website. Depending on the major, there is often a lack of resources. Tutoring is scarce, and it can be hard to find help.
